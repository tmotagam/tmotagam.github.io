Hi, I’m Motagamwala Taha Arif Ali — but online, I go by tmotagam. I’m a developer with a passion for building practical, real-world software solutions. I’ve created a range of open source applications including the Store Management System, Tahafacex, FTPT, and Online Protracted Examination (OPE). These projects focus on simplifying business processes, enhancing digital interaction, and improving operational efficiency. I also enjoy working on open-source tools and libraries that help other developers. I maintain sqlite-electron, a Node.js package for seamless SQLite integration in Electron apps, and Secweb, a Python library aimed at building secure web applications.

If you have any questions about me, my projects, or potential opportunities — including freelance work or full-time roles — feel free to reach out. I’m always open to connecting, collaborating, or exploring new challenges.
