Face Recognition Meets Simplicity:

TahafaceX is a smart, offline attendance app that uses face recognition to make tracking attendance effortless and accurate. Whether you're in a classroom, on a construction site, or out in the field, TahafaceX works anywhere — no internet required. It's fast, secure, and designed to make attendance as simple as showing up. Simple, secure, and portable, TahafaceX is built to save you time and eliminate the hassle of traditional attendance tracking.


Smart, Secure, and User-Friendly Features:

Creating No-Hassle Attendance Records

Effortless, accurate tracking with face recognition — no manual input, no internet, no stress.

Mark Attendance Using Face Recognition Technology

Just show up — TahafaceX captures and records attendance in seconds, no taps or typing needed.

Manage Your Group with Ease

Whether it’s a team, class, or department — organize and track attendance effortlessly in one place.

Tailored for Students, Teams, and Everyone in Between

TahafaceX adapts to your setup — schools, businesses, or beyond.

Backup and Restore — Your Data, Your Control

Easily back up and restore your attendance records right on your device. No cloud involved — you decide where your data goes.
