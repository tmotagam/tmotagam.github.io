Smarter, Safer Online Exams — Anywhere, Anytime:

A secure and user-friendly platform for creating and conducting online exams. Examinees can take exams directly from their Windows laptops or desktops—no extra devices or test centers required. The system features built-in AI that monitors and analyzes exam recordings to detect potential cheating. Flagged incidents are reviewed by human moderators, whose decisions are taken into account to ensure fairness. All exam data is securely stored.


Secure. Reliable. Transparent. Explore What OPE Offers:

  • Open Source & Community-Driven
    Our platform is fully open source, transparent, customizable, and built by a growing community on github. You have the freedom to adapt it to your institution’s needs, with no vendor lock-in or hidden fees.
  • Smart AI Proctoring + Human Review
    Our AI watches out for cheating by monitoring video, and audio activity. If something looks suspicious, it flags it for review by real human moderators to ensure every decision is fair and balanced.
  • Kiosk Mode for Focused Testing
    The OPE software temporarily converts the test-taker’s device into a secure kiosk. This means no switching tabs, screen sharing, or background apps—just a dedicated, distraction-free exam experience.
  • Audio & Video Monitoring
    We record both webcam video and microphone audio during the test. This helps identify unauthorized assistance, ensures exam integrity, and provides clear evidence in case of disputes.
  • Secure & Private by Design
    All exam data—recordings, questions, and results is encrypted and securely stored. We follow strict privacy standards and only use data for exam monitoring and fairness. You stay in control of your information.
