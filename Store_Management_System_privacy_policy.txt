2. Information We Collect

The Store Management System App collects the following information for its functioning:

Name

Store Registration Number

Store Address

This data is manually entered by the user and stored locally on the device. The App does not require an internet connection to function, and all data is stored in a folder on the device called sms_database located in your "Documents" folder.
